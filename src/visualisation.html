The following data used is the Innovation Scorecard (NICE Technology Appraisals in the NHS in England) to October 2024 at the ICB level. If you’re not familiar with this dataset, it is found here (“Background and Introduction | NHSBSA” (n.d.)) and “aims to improve transparency within the NHS of what treatments recommended by NICE are available at a local level. The local level can be within trusts and ICBs as well as national and NHS England region levels. The Innovation Scorecard has been published with the intention of assisting the NHS in the identification of variation which, through discussion, can be explained, challenged, or acted upon. It is not intended to be used for performance management.”.

dat1 %>% filter(high_level_condition == "Breast Cancer") %>%  ggplot(aes(x = treatment_name, y = value)) +
  geom_boxplot(fill = "lightblue", alpha = 0.7) +
  theme_minimal() +
  labs(title = "Distribution of Utilisation by Treatment Type within Breast Cancer",
       y = "DDD per 100,000 population",
       x = "Treatment Type") +
  theme(axis.text.x = element_text(angle = 45, hjust = 1))
dat1 %>%
  group_by(provider_name) %>%
  summarize(avg_utilization = mean(value)) %>%
  arrange(desc(avg_utilization)) %>%
  head(10) %>%
  ggplot(aes(x = reorder(provider_name, avg_utilization), y = avg_utilization)) +
  geom_point() +
  coord_flip() +
  theme_minimal() +
  labs(title = "Top 10 Providers by Average Utilization",
       x = "Provider Name",
       y = "Average DDD per 100,000 population")
